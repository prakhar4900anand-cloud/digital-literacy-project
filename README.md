 Project Overview
 
The objective of this program is to help students gain knowledge and skills to develop digital literacy regarding safe internet access, creating a digital identity, developing coding skills, and knowing about cyber threats like UPI fraud. 

There will be many types of activities in this project. There will be infographics, portfolios, coding tasks, questionnaires, and writing professional emails.

---

Objectives

Realizing how important digital literacy is having an understanding of how to use the internet in a safe and responsible manner, having an online portfolio ( Github/LinkedIn), using coding platforms to help you solve problems better, as well as becoming knowledgeable about cybercrime and how to prevent it.

Cyber Safety Tips:
- Never share your OTPs or UPI PINs with anyone - seriously, not even if they claim to be from your bank.
- Avoid clicking unknown links.
- Always double-check payment requests before hitting confirm - it's worth the extra few seconds.
- Stick to apps you know and trust - if you haven't heard of it, maybe skip it.
- Set up your alert notifications for transactions.
