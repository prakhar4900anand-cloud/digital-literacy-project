Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

DOs and DONTs for using social media

1) DO: Be sure to use secure, complex passwords.
2) DO: Refrain from posting anything on your profile that is personal in nature. 
3) DO: Research every piece of information before reposting it.
4) DO: Be considerate to those that you interact with via social media.
5) DO: Use the platform's privacy settings to protect your account. 

1) DONT: Share your personal information (including your password or your OTP) to anyone.
2) DONT: Post inappropriate material.
3) DONT: Click on unknown links. 
4) DONT: Distribute misinformation (i.e., false news).
5) DONT: Give away excessive personal information about yourself.
